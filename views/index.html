champPoolDB

A leightweight REST API to query your League of Legends champion pool.


INTRODUCTION

Any good League of Legends player needs a reasonably large champion pool. Ideally, you have 3-4 champions per role you're really good at. This helps you to counterpick, adapt to your team, and be a equally good player across different metas. This little tool is meant to help you track your champion pool, give you stats on how well you mastered a specific champ, and which other champs would be worth exploring based on your playstyle. Please keep in mind that this is work in progress - any feedback is much appreciated.


FEATURES

You can query the API with the following options:

GET

GET "/me" All your champions inside your pool.

GET "/me?tags=tank" All your tanks.

GET "/all" All champions in the whole game.

GET "/all?tags=mage" All mages in the whole game.

GET "/all?name=ekko" Everything about Ekko.

POST

POST "/add/:id" Adds a champion based on ID to your personal DB.
